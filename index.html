The Wedding

Shot on a VHS camcorder by my 68 year old uncle, I took 47 minutes worth of 4:3 SD video and cut it down to a music video that clocks in at about 7 minutes.

The Silly-versary

Chronicling 17 years of "Relationship Status: In a Relationship." On September 16 (or 17, depending on who you ask), 1997, Tia and I made it official. I took a bunch of photos that had been scanned into the interwebs and married them to music.

MUSIC CREDIT: "Water into Wine" by Dave Matthews Band
